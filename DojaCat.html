Doja Cat

21/10/1995

About her

Doja Cat is an American rapper, singer, and songwriter known for her unique blend of hip-hop, R\&B, and pop sounds. She first gained attention with her viral song Mooo! and later rose to fame with hits like Say so and Kiss me more. Doja Cat is praised for her bold style, creative music videos, and ability to switch between rap and singing seamlessly. She has become one of the most influential female artists of her generation.

Important events

  1. 2018 - Viral Breakthrough: Doja Cat went viral with her playful track “Mooo!”, which introduced her to a global audience.
  2. 2020 - “Say So” Success:Her song “Say So” topped the Billboard Hot 100, becoming her first number-one hit.
  3. 2021 - Grammy Win:She won a Grammy Award for “Kiss Me More” with SZA, marking a major milestone.
  4. 2023 - Scarlet Album:She released her album Scarlet, showcasing a darker, more experimental side of her artistry.

Musical awards:

  • Grammy Award– Best Pop Duo/Group Performance (Kiss Me More with SZA).
  • Billboard Music Awards 2022– 4 wins, including Top R&B Artist and Top R&B Album.
  • BMI Pop Music Awards 2023- Songwriter of the Year.
  • American Music Awards- multiple wins, including Favorite Female R&B Artist.
  • MTV Video Music Awards (VMAs)- several awards, including Best New Artist.
